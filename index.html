Tikka Indian Cuisine

Featuring Fresh Indian Cuisine

Featuring Authentic Indian Cuisine in a modern, family run atmosphere with friendly service. We also offer full service catering, and daily lunch specials. We look forward to having you as our guests. Tikka Indian Cuisine is where your senses come alive with the aroma of fresh Indian spices in the air. Come enjoy and explore the flavors of our Indian food. You will delight in visual creations that entice the eyes and enliven the palette. Our vision is to reflect the authentic dishes of India at Tikka Indian Cuisine, with ambiance, service, and modern presentation. We welcome you to embark on a culinary journey as you enjoy the ultimate dining experience and invite you to experience Tikka Indian Cuisine.
